Reading

I have always loved reading and now am lucky enough to get to teach others how to read! See below for a few of my favorite books, old and new!

  • The Lincoln Highway by Amor Towles
    • My favorite Amor Towles book! Two brothers embark on a road trip across America dreaming of a grand future but end up on a journey of self-discovery.
  • The Day the Crayons Quit by Drew Daywalt
    • My favorite children's book. Duncan's crayons go on strike! Each crayon writes a letter outlining their grievances. P.S. A bit long for one read aloud, best to split into two days :)
  • The Fellowship of Puzzlemakers by Samuel Burr
    • A lighthearted read about a baby left on the steps of an unorthodox group of puzzle fanatics. Full of fun riddles, puzzles, and an emphasis on building relationships!
  • Nothing to See Here by Kevin Wilson
    • A coming of age book about teenagers testing limits and finding themselves, with a hint of mystery. A quick relatable read!
